GUESS MY NUMBER

Pig Game:

"Pig Game is an entertaining dice game that brings laughter and excitement to any gathering. The objective of the game is to accumulate points by rolling a dice, but beware of the pig! Roll a '1', and your turn ends, losing all your accumulated points for that round. The game offers strategic decision-making opportunities: Do you risk rolling again for more points or play it safe and pass the turn to the next player? With its simple rules and quick gameplay, Pig Game is a perfect choice for parties, family game nights, or even a fun way to pass the time alone. Get ready to roll the dice and enjoy the thrill of Pig Game!"

Memory Match:

"Memory Match is a classic and addictive game that tests and enhances your memory skills. The game presents a grid of facedown cards, each containing a hidden image. Your task is to find and match pairs of cards by flipping them over, relying on your memory to remember their locations. Memory Match offers different difficulty levels, allowing you to challenge yourself and improve your memory capacity. The game features pleasant graphics and soothing sounds, creating a relaxing and immersive experience. Whether you're a child or an adult, Memory Match is a fantastic way to train your brain and have a great time!"

Rock Paper Scissors:

"Rock Paper Scissors is a timeless game of strategy, luck, and anticipation. It's a simple yet captivating game where two players compete by simultaneously forming one of three hand gestures: rock, paper, or scissors. The rules are straightforward: rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. The game offers endless possibilities for mind games and strategic thinking as you try to predict your opponent's move while choosing your own. With its quick rounds and easy-to-understand rules, Rock Paper Scissors is an ideal game for impromptu matches with friends or to settle friendly disputes. Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a classic game played on a 3x3 grid. Two players take turns placing their symbol (X or O) in empty squares on the grid. The goal is to create a line of three matching symbols either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The first player to achieve this wins the game. If all squares are filled without a winner, the game ends in a tie. It's a simple and strategic game that can be played quickly and enjoyed by players of all ages.

Memory Match

Memory Match is a game where you flip over pairs of cards to find matching images. The objective is to remember the positions of the cards and uncover all the pairs. Keep flipping two cards at a time, and if they match, you earn a point and get to continue. The game ends when all the pairs are found, and the player with the most points wins. It's a stimulating and enjoyable game that tests your memory and concentration

Hangman

Hangman is a word-guessing game where one player thinks of a word and the other player tries to guess it. The word is represented by dashes, each representing a letter. The guessing player suggests letters one at a time, and if the letter is in the word, it is revealed in the corresponding positions. If the guessed letter is not in the word, a part of the "hangman" is drawn. The guessing player has a limited number of incorrect guesses before the hangman is completed and they lose the game. The goal is to guess the word before the hangman is fully drawn
